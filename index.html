    The Migratory Patterns and Social Behavior of Geese

    Geese, belonging to the family Anatidae, are remarkable birds known for their strong migratory instincts and complex social structures. There are several species of geese, including the well-known Canada goose (Branta canadensis) and the Greylag goose (Anser anser). These birds are found across various parts of the world and are highly adapted to both temperate and polar regions.
